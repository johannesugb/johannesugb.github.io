For those of you currently starting with Vulkan development, you might find my Introduction to Vulkan lecture and the Vulkan Workshop helpful.

For your subsequent C++ and Vulkan programming adventures, I might have a suitable framework for you: Gears-Vk can let you get into gears quickly with Visual Studio on Windows. For development on other platforms, you might find Auto-Vk useful.

I currently devote most of my time to real-time rendering research at the TU Wien's Research Unit of Computer Graphics and to further the development of Gears-Vk and Auto-Vk. However, my most famous GitHub repository is VolumetricLinesUnity, an asset for Unity3D that renders lines in a volumetric appearance.
